About Us

Yashoda Technical Campus, located in Satara, Maharashtra was established in 2011. The institute offers UG, PG and After 10th courses in IT & Software, Engineering, Medicine & Health Sciences and Business & Management Studies with specialisations including Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Pharmaceutical, Pharmacology, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning and Electronics & Communication Engineering. B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, B.Pharma, M.E./M.Tech, M.Pharma, After 10th Diploma, D.Pharma and MCA are among the popular flagship courses. The institute offers this program in Full Time mode. The fees of IT & Software, Engineering, Medicine & Health Sciences and Business & Management Studies courses at the Yashoda Technical Campus ranges between INR 12,000-3,74,560. The infrastructure of the institute provides a better learning environment for the students.
