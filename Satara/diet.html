About Us

Dnyanshree Institute of Engineering and Technology is located in Satara, Maharashtra and was established in 2012. It is a premier institute in higher education and offers UG and After 10th in Full Time mode in the form of Diploma and Degree programmes. The most popular programmes offered at Dnyanshree Institute of Engineering and Technology are B.E. / B.Tech, After 10th Diploma and UG Diploma programmes. The institute offers AICTE approved programs to students in Full Time mode to pursue their careers in higher studies. Further, the institute offers a wide range of programmes in the stream of Engineering. The institute has an intake capacity of 315 seats for all of its programmes. Dnyanshree Institute of Engineering and Technology has a panel of experienced lecturers to inspire students in their respective careers and to give them tips and invaluable insights in every course. The institute also offers an excellent infrastructure.
