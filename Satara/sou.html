About Us

Located in Satara, Maharashtra, SOU Devibai Narayandas Chhabada Rural Education Society offers several popular courses such as B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, B.Pharma, After 10th Diploma, D.Pharma, UG Diploma etc. The institute offers courses in fields such as Engineering, Medicine & Health Sciences, Nursing and Business & Management Studies, mostly in Full Time mode. It offers various UG, PG and After 10th level courses within its modern infrastructure and facilities for the students. SOU Devibai Narayandas Chhabada Rural Education Society offers courses ranging around INR 12,000-3,34,000, depending on the program candidates opt for.
