About Us

Arvind Gavali College of Engineering was established in year 2010 and is located in Satara, Maharashtra. It offers courses at UG, PG and After 10th levels with highly experienced faculty in Full Time mode. Approved by the AICTE, the institute offers multiple courses such as B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, After 10th Diploma and UG Diploma courses. Furthermore, it offers several specialisations in the field of Engineering. Arvind Gavali College of Engineering offers these courses within the range of INR 48,000-2,57,772 with the intent to provide education at highly affordable fees. The institute offers 738 seats, encouraging more candidates to gain knowledge and enhance their skills.
