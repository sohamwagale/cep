About Us

Dadasaheb Mokashi College of Food Technology, located in Satara, Maharashtra, is a renowned educational institution and was established in the year 2004. The institute offers a wide range of Degree programmes at UG and PG levels. These courses are offered across various fields, including Engineering. Some of the most popular programme offered at the institute are B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech. The institute strives to provide quality education to students at an affordable fee to make it accessible for meritorious students. The institute has a group of experienced and professional faculty members to offer valuable education to students. In addition to this, the institute campus offers a modern infrastructure to students and caters to all their needs.
