About Us

Located in Satara, Maharashtra, College of Engineering and Technology (Degree), Abhinav Education Society was established in 2009. College of Engineering and Technology (Degree), Abhinav Education Society, a leading institute in higher education, offers Degree courses to students. These courses are provided at the UG level across Engineering stream. The institute offers courses in Full Time mode. The institute is famous for offering courses such as B.E. / B.Tech. College of Engineering and Technology (Degree), Abhinav Education Society students are taught by faculty who are trained professionals in fields such as Computer Science Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. To avail of this quality education, students must pay fee ranging between INR 51,351-1,44,140. With this institute, students can enjoy studying in the state of the art infrastructure.
