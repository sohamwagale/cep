About Us

Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil College of Engineering is located in Satara, Maharashtra and was established in 1983. It is a premier institute in higher education and offers UG and PG in Full Time mode in the form of Degree programmes. The most popular programmes offered at Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil College of Engineering are B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech programmes. The institute offers AICTE approved programs to students in Full Time mode to pursue their careers in higher studies. Further, the institute offers a wide range of programmes in the stream of Engineering. The institute has an intake capacity of 420 seats for all of its programmes. Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil College of Engineering has a panel of experienced lecturers to inspire students in their respective careers and to give them tips and invaluable insights in every course. The institute also offers an excellent infrastructure
