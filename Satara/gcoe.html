About Us

Located in Satara, Maharashtra, Government College of Engineering, Karad is a prominent institute that offers Degree courses in Full Time mode. These courses are offered across varied streams, namely Engineering and IT & Software. The course fee at this institute ranges between INR 84,004-2,33,814. The faculty of the institute is highly experienced and provides students with expert guidance. Government College of Engineering, Karad also offers well-equipped campus facilities for the students.
