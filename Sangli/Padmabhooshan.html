About Us

Padmabhooshan Vasantraodada Patil Institute of Technology, Budhgaon was established in year 1983 and is located in Sangli, Maharashtra. It offers courses at UG, PG and After 10th levels with highly experienced faculty in Full Time mode. Approved by the AICTE, the institute offers multiple courses such as B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech and After 10th Diploma courses. Furthermore, it offers several specialisations in the field of Engineering. Padmabhooshan Vasantraodada Patil Institute of Technology, Budhgaon offers these courses within the range of INR 18,000-2,99,028 with the intent to provide education at highly affordable fees. The institute offers 1416 seats, encouraging more candidates to gain knowledge and enhance their skills.
