About Us

Walchand College of Engineering (WCE), established in 1947, is ranked 168th among the engineering colleges in India by the NIRF 2020 rankings. The college awards Diploma, UG, PG and Ph.D. courses in engineering which are recognized by AICTE. Eligibility for UG and PG courses is 10 + 2 passed and bachelorâ€™s degrees passed respectively. Admissions to all the courses is done based on an entrance examination and through CAP. Graduates from the college are placed in companies such as Linkedin, Amazon, Dell, Infosys, HSBC, HCL, TCS, etc. with an average salary package of INR 5 LPA. The highest salary package at the college goes up to INR 33 LPA as per the 2019 placements. Walchand College of Engineering has 153 Rankings by NIRF in the year 2019.
