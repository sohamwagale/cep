About Us

Nanasaheb Mahadik College of Engineering, located in Sangli, Maharashtra, is a renowned educational institution and was established in the year 2011. The institute offers a wide range of Degree programmes at UG level. The institute offers AICTE approved programs to students in Full Time mode to pursue their careers in higher studies. These courses are offered across various fields, including Engineering. One of the most popular programme offered at the institute is B.E. / B.Tech. The institute strives to provide quality education to students at an affordable fee to make it accessible for meritorious students. The institute has a group of experienced and professional faculty members to offer valuable education to students. In addition to this, the institute campus offers a modern infrastructure to students and caters to all their needs.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology in Civil Engineering
