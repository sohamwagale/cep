About Us

RIT Islampur offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in several specializations under business and engineering departments. It offers BTech, MTech, and Diploma courses to working professionals as well. Admissions to BTech, MTech, and MBA are granted on the basis of national level entrance examinations such as JEE Main / MHT CET, GATE and MAH-CET / ATMA / CAT / CMAT and as per the guidelines of AICTE and DTE. From the acdemic session 2024-25, RIT Islampur is launching new and trending courses including BBA, BCA, MCA, and diploma in mechatronics, admission to which is based on MAH CET. The institute has also had a very impressive placement record. In 2024, over 311 BTech students were placed and the highest package reported for MBA and BTech is INR 9 LPA and INR 7.5 LPA respectively.

Our Facilities

  • RIT Islampur Facilities Hostel: Hostel facilities are provided to male and female students. Gym facility, Tea/Coffee vending machine, high-speed internet, TVs in all the messes, and daily Newspapers are some of the facilities provided to hostel residents. Within the campus, RIT Islampur has 08 hostel buildings (4 for boys and 4 for girls) accommodating 486 girls and 696 boys. There are 219 rooms for boys and 139 rooms for girls which are either three-sharing or four-sharing. The hostel fee is INR 26,000 for boys and INR 30,500 for girls, and the mess fees is INR 2400 for boys and INR 2200 for girls. RIT Islampur hostels have 08 spacious dining halls, a canteen, 04 snack centers & 01 cafeteria. Health care is also provided 24/7 to all the students in the campus alomg with 24/7 electricity, guest house, playgrounds, hot water facility, a laundry unit, separate gym for girls, etc. Library: The library at RIT Islampur is accessible to students and faculty members for academic and research purposes. It is a four-storied air conditioned separate building with 1,35,041 + print and e-books, 33,251+ reference books, 209 + print national and international engineering and management journals, 36302+ electronic journals and magazines, 27352 + e-books and online databases. Transportation: RIT provides transport facility to its students in nine exclusive buses. It connects Karad, Miraj, Kolhapur and Palus to RIT. Sports: The institute has a badminton court, basketball court, and kho kho court for the students. It also has a gymkhana with all kinds of fitness equipment available for use by the students. Medical: A dispensary in the campus takes care of preliminary free medical treatment. An ambulance is always ready to meet any medical emergency.
