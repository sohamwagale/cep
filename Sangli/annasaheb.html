About Us

Located in Sangli, Maharashtra, Annasaheb Dange College of Engineering and Technology was established in 1999. Annasaheb Dange College of Engineering and Technology, a leading institute in higher education, offers Degree courses to students. These courses are provided at the UG and PG level across Engineering stream. The institute offers courses in Full Time mode. The institute is famous for offering courses such as B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech. Annasaheb Dange College of Engineering and Technology students are taught by faculty who are trained professionals in fields such as Computer Science Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Structural Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Agriculture & Farm Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Food Technology and Mechanical Engineering. To avail of this quality education, students must pay fee ranging between INR 1,80,000-3,73,916. With this institute, students can enjoy studying in the state of the art infrastructure.
