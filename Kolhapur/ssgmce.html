About Us

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering (SSGMCE) in Kolhapur is dedicated to providing high-quality engineering education, fostering innovation, and cultivating an environment where students can thrive. SSGMCE stands out for its experienced faculty, advanced labs, and a commitment to student success.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication
  • Master of Engineering in Power Electronics

Our Facilities

  • Modern Library with Digital Resources
  • Student Hostel and Cafeteria
  • Sports Complex and Gym
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi Campus
