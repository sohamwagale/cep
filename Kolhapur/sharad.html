About Us

Sharad Institute of Technology College of Engineering was founded in 2009 by Shri. Shamrao Patil (Yadravkar) Educational & Charitable Trust, Jaysingpur District-Kolhapur, which was founded by Late Shri Shamrao Patil (Yadravkar), 1986. Shri Anil Bagane is the Executive Director of Sharad Institute of Technology, reputed Industrialist and Vice Chairman of Parvati Industrial Estate & Manufacturing Association, Yadrav (PIEMA) Shri Rajendra Patil, fouinder Chairman Sharad Institute of Technology.
