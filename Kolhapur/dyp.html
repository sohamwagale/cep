About Us

DY Patil College of Engineering Kolhapur is a self-financing autonomous college which was established in 1984. The college is approved by AICTE and is affiliated with the Shivaji University, Kolhapur. The college is accredited with A Grade by NAAC. The college provides 8 UG courses and two PG courses to students. These courses are provided across Engineering and various other streams. D Y Patil College Kolhapur is also accredited by NBA and recognised by the Government of Maharashtra. D. Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology offers BTech and B.Arch as flagship programmes to students. DY Patil College Kolhapur has a state of the art campus with multiple facilities available in the college. The college has a wifi enabled campus with 24 hours internet connectivity. D.Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology, Kolhapur has sports facilities. Located in Kolhapur (Maharashtra), this reputed college is ranked 192 by India Today 2024 under the Top Engineering (Pvt) Colleges in India category and 222 by India Today under the Top Engineering Colleges in India 2024 category.
